The Best Movies According To Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spider-Man: Across The Spider-Verse

The animation on this movie is godly along with the story telling.

The Bee Movie

I've seen this movie probably about 100 times and it never gets old. (And no, I'm not joking)

John Wick 4

The stunts are great, along with the countless fight scenes. I also really like the story, although that's not what most people watch John Wick for.

I would also like to add that many more movies could've gone in this list.
